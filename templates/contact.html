Frequently Asked Questions

Simply browse available hostels, click "View Details" on your preferred option, and fill out the booking form.

Is there a booking fee?

No, our platform is completely free for students. You only pay the hostel directly.

Can I cancel my booking?

Cancellation policies vary by hostel. Contact the hostel directly or reach out to us for assistance.
